Profile

Name

Born

Douglas, Isle of Man

Team

Bahrain McLaren

Specialty

Sprinter

Mark has had a distinguished career to date, both on the track and on the road and is widely regarded as one of the best sprinters of all time.

Having signed his first professional contract in 2006, Mark announced himself on the international road racing scene in 2008 taking 2 stages in the Giro, followed by an incredible 4 stages at the Tour de France.
